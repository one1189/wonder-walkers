Useful Gear

We have recommened some items that we personally have used on our treks. Being outdoors for so long, we have picked up a thing or two on what can be useful on the road. From footwear, to water bottles, Wonder Walkers has you covered!



Whilst these don't look like your typical hiking shoes, the Vibram v-trek offers complete support for you feet.

By allowing your feet to maintain its natural shape, your comfort levels will be surprisingly high for your treks.


The importance of hydration whilst out and about is so vital, especially for the more difficult journeys.

I strongly recommend a camel pack such as the Osprey Skarab. It cuts the need for any water bottles with a 2.5ltr fluid capacity, and doubles as a backpack.


Offering trekkers that extra push, these poles are lightweight at just over 1lb per pair. They are also collapsable, to minimalise how much you need to carry.


All these items are personable to us here at Wonder Walkers, and equipment we highly recommend. However The Hiking Guy explores some other options.
